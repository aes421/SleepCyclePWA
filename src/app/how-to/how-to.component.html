Rested gives you personalized suggestions to help you find the perfect bedtime.


According to experts, your body goes through multiple sleep cycles each night lasting around 90 minutes each. If you wake up in the middle of a sleep cycle you're likely to feel groggy and tired, but if you wake up at the end of sleep cycle you'll feel refreshed and rested.


Remember, on average it takes 10-20 minutes to fall asleep, so make sure you get in bed early.
